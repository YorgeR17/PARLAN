Who is Parlan and what is our mission?

You might be asking yourself who or what is Parlan? Parlan is a service for advertising local and global land party events. We provide the service of offering our website space as a way to show off companies, big groups, or small group land parties. With a rating system to show how most of these groups or organizations are rated among the community. Are ad space isn't just a boring old ad you can block with an adblock plugin. As the ad is embedded into the web pages code. Along with our advertisement service we offer admission tickets to land parties, were your able to reserve your ticket for admission to your desired land party. But that's not all Parlan has to offer. Parlan offers a game streaming service, so no matter where you are and as long as you have a internet connection, you are able to stream your favorite multiplayer games. The Parlan game streaming service offers two types of tickets. In which the first option is a 24hrs access to the game library with twenty access codes for twenty different users. As for option two is a 24hrs access with unlimited amount of access with unlimited amount of users. Our parlan gaming library consist of two hundred multiplier games, to insure that you and your group are able to game together.
